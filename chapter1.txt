"The Theme of King JJ" is a Japanese animated series belonging to a collection of animated series called "Yuri On Ice". Settling in the same timeline as the events of Yuri on Ice after the Grand Prix Finals, it dives into the perspective and life of Jean-Jacques Leroy after his disastrous short program as he is forced to confront his past and encounters lots of ups and downs as he develops into a better person. Despite the initial criticism and low ratings at the start of the show, "The Theme of King JJ" will eventually become one of the most critically-acclaimed and beloved series of all the series, with many fans praising JJ's realistic character development and its portrayal of mental health disorders, such as ADHD and anxiety.
